# Pokemon TCG Format Legality Reference

## Regulation Marks

Every Pokemon TCG card printed since Sword & Shield base has a regulation mark (letter) in the bottom-left corner. This mark determines format legality.

| H | Scarlet & Violet late / Next gen | Surging Sparks (SSP), Prismatic Evolutions (PRE), Journey Together (JTG), Black Bolt (BLK), White Flare (WHT), Mega Evolution (MEG), Phantasmal Flames (PFL), Ascended Heroes (ASC), Perfect Order (POR) | Currently legal in Standard |

## Standard Format

- **Legal regulation marks:** G and H
- **Rotation:** Annual, typically announced in spring, effective mid-year
- **Deck rules:** 60 cards, max 4 copies of same-name card (except Basic Energy)
- **Ace Spec:** Max 1 Ace Spec card per deck
- **Radiant Pokemon:** Max 1 Radiant Pokemon per deck (note: most Radiant Pokemon are mark E/F, so currently rotated out of Standard)
- **VSTAR Power:** Once per game per player
- **Use this format for:** Competitive play, official tournaments, Pokemon TCG Live ranked ladder

## Expanded Format

- **Legal regulation marks:** B through H (all marks B and later)
- **No annual rotation** (only bans)
- **Deck rules:** Same as Standard (60 cards, max 4 copies)
- **Use this format for:** Wider card pool, more combos, some regional events, PTCGL Expanded ladder

## Unlimited Format

- **All cards ever printed are legal**
- **No banned list**
- **Casual play only** — not used in official tournaments
- **Use this format for:** Fun, nostalgia, kitchen table
