# Pokemon Type Chart Reference

## Type Effectiveness Multipliers
- Super Effective: 2x damage
- Not Very Effective: 0.5x damage  
- Immune: 0x damage
- Neutral: 1x damage
- Dual types stack: SE + SE = 4x, NVE + NVE = 0.25x, SE + NVE = 1x

## Common Defensive Synergy Cores

### Fire / Water / Grass
Each resists what the others are weak to. Classic and reliable.

### Fairy / Dragon / Steel  
Fairy beats Dragon, Dragon resists Steel's weaknesses, Steel resists Fairy's weaknesses. (Historically called the "Fantasy Core")

### Flying / Electric
Flying is immune to Ground (Electric's weakness), Electric hits Water/Flying (Flying's weaknesses).

## Key Immunities for VGC
- Ground immunity: Flying types, Levitate ability, Air Balloon item
- Ghost immunity: Normal types
- Normal/Fighting immunity: Ghost types
- Electric immunity: Ground types, Lightning Rod, Volt Absorb, Motor Drive
- Water immunity: Water Absorb, Storm Drain, Dry Skin (partial)
- Fire immunity: Flash Fire
- Grass immunity: Sap Sipper
